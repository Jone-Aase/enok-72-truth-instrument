## SKIP 2 — HMS Resolution (William Wales, desember 1774)

**Arkiv:** Cambridge University Library — Board of Longitude Papers, RGO 14/58  
**Kilde:** [https://cudl.lib.cam.ac.uk/view/MS-RGO-00014-00058/337](https://cudl.lib.cam.ac.uk/view/MS-RGO-00014-00058/337)  
**IIIF bilde-URL:** [https://images.lib.cam.ac.uk/iiif/MS-RGO-00014-00058-000-00337.jp2/full/1200,/0/default.jpg](https://images.lib.cam.ac.uk/iiif/MS-RGO-00014-00058-000-00337.jp2/full/1200,/0/default.jpg)  
**Folio:** 144r (seq 337)  
**Fil i workspace:** `wales_resolution_1774_dec20_terra-del-fuego.jpg`

**Kontekst:** William Wales var astronom på Cooks 2. verdensreise (1772–1775). Loggen "On board his Majesty's Sloop Resolution" for **tirsdag 20. desember 1774** beskriver ankomst til Terra del Fuego. Wales skriver i egne ord:

> *"On computing my Observations I found that the Latitude of the Place was 55°21' 57 ½... Kendal's Watch gave the Longitude 290°19' 41½, reckoning from New Zealand"*

Og videre:
> *"All this part of the Coast of Terra del Fuego, as far as we have yet passed, is broken..."*

**Koordinater:**
- **Breddegrad:** 55°21'57½"S (observert)
- **Lengdegrad:** 290°19'41½" fra New Zealand = ca. 69°40'W fra Greenwich
- Oppsummeringsrad viser: Lat DR 55°32½S, Long DR 287°41½, Long by K 290°26½

**Merknad:** Siden er skrevet for hånd av Wales selv og viser kolonner for "Latit. D.R.", "Latit. Obserd.", "Long. D.R.", "Long by K[endal's chronometer]", "Long by A[rnold]", "Long by Obs. Baron[ometer]". CC BY-NC 4.0 lisens, Cambridge University Library.

## SKIP 3 — HMS Centurion (George Anson, mars 1741)

**Arkiv:** Internet Archive — *A Voyage Round the World* (1790-utgaven)  
**Identifier:** `bim_eighteenth-century_a-voyage-round-the-world_anson-george-anson-bar_1790_1`  
**Kilde:** [https://archive.org/details/bim_eighteenth-century_a-voyage-round-the-world_anson-george-anson-bar_1790_1](https://archive.org/details/bim_eighteenth-century_a-voyage-round-the-world_anson-george-anson-bar_1790_1)  
**Scan:** 0085 (bokside 86), Chapter VII  
**Fil i workspace:** `anson_centurion_1741_cape-virgin-mary_magellan.jpg`

**Tekst på siden (tydelig lesbart i trykt 1790-utgave):**

> *"On the 4th of March, we were in sight of **Cape Virgin Mary**, and not more than six or seven leagues distant from it. This cape is the northern boundary of the entrance of the **Straits of Magellan**; it lies in the **latitude of 52° : 21' south**, and **longitude from London, 71° 44' west**, and seems to be a low flat land ending in a point."*

**Koordinater:**
- **Sted:** Cape Virgin Mary (nordre inngang til Magellanstredet)
- **Breddegrad:** 52°21'S
- **Lengdegrad:** 71°44'W fra London
- **Dato:** 4. mars 1741

**Merknad:** Anson kommanderte en eskadre på 6 skip som skulle angripe spanske kolonier. De kjempet seg forbi Kapp Horn med store tap. Cape Virgin Mary er det nordre markeringspunktet for Magellanstredet. Boken er en trykt reisebeskrivelse (1790-utgave av den opprinnelige 1748-utgaven), skannet i høyoppløsning — 4795×7778 piksler.
