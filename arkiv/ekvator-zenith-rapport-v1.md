# Ekvator-markører: zenith-bevis rangert
## Verifisering av "solen 90° rett opp" på jevndøgn

**Premiss for Enoks modell**: Et punkt er kun en ekte ekvator-markør dersom det er dokumentert observasjon av solen rett opp i zenit (ingen skygge fra gnomon) ved jevndøgn (mars/september).

---

## Rangering

### KATEGORI A — Direkte zenith-bevis fra gammel observasjon

#### EKV-01 Catequilla (0.00000°N, 78.42861°W)
**Status**: STERKEST BEVIS — direkte zenith-observasjon dokumentert

**Bevis**:
- "When the sun was in zenith passage on the autumnal equinox the gnome in the center of the circular platform had no shadow. It is for this reason that the huaca is still given ritual offerings at this time of the year." ([San Catequilla de Pichincha — tDAR/academia.edu](https://core.tdar.org/document/396903/san-catequilla-de-pichincha-and-catequil-the-cult-to-lightning-illapa-in-the-context-of-inca-expansion))
- "The most highly venerated gnome was Catequilla de Pichincha, primarily because when the sun was overhead during certain parts of the annual cycle there was increasingly diminished shadow around the pillar or gnome at this lightning huaca." ([OUP Blog — Earthquake at the lightning huaca](https://blog.oup.com/2014/09/earthquake-lightning-huaca-san-catequilla-de-pichincha/))
- "There is only one place where objects cast no shadow at midday on the equinoxes: the equator. So the Quitsa-to Project proposes that careful observation of shadows would have indicated the equator's location to the ancients." ([JW.org — Amazing Discoveries at Earth's Equator](https://www.jw.org/en/library/magazines/g20051222/Amazing-Discoveries-at-Earths-Equator/))
- Geodetisk verifikasjon med satellitt-RTK: ekliptikkvinkelen 23.49° matcher orienteringen av ruinene ([Dialnet — Arqueoastronomía y Geodesia, Catequilla](https://dialnet.unirioja.es/servlet/articulo?codigo=8460337))

**Alder**: ca. 800 e.Kr. (Quitu-Cara-kulturen, før-inka)
**Kildevurdering**: Sterk — arkeologisk huaca med gnomon-plattform i bruk i over 1000 år, fortsatt med rituelle ofringer ved jevndøgn

#### EKV-05 Pontianak — Tugu Khatulistiwa (0.00000°N, 109.32278°E)
**Status**: STERKT BEVIS — dokumentert solkulminasjon i bruk

**Bevis**:
- "One of the most spectacular attractions of the Equator Monument is the occurrence of the solar culmination phenomenon. This moment occurs when the sun is directly above the equator. When the moment occurs, the shadows of all objects that stand upright become 'lost' because they fall right under the object itself. This phenomenon occurs twice a year, between March 21-23 and September 21-23." ([Indonesia.travel — Tugu Khatulistiwa](https://www.indonesia.travel/gb/en/travel-ideas/culture/tugu-khatulistiwa/))
- "Make sure you visit this monument at the right time so that you can see the unique of natural phenomenon at this location. When the sun has the right position on the equator, the sun culmination will occur. At the moment, you will not be able to see the shadow objects, as well as the shadow of the monument which will disappear in a few moments." ([Indonesia-tourism.com — Tugu Katulistiwa](https://www.indonesia-tourism.com/west-kalimantan/tugu_katulistiwa.html))
- Original monument bygget 1928 ([Wikipedia — Equator Monument Pontianak](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Equator_Monument))

**Alder**: monument fra 1928, men solkulminasjons-fenomenet er observerbart og brukes som turistattraksjon årlig
**Kildevurdering**: Sterk — fenomenet er dokumentert i flere uavhengige kilder og verifisert med målinger to ganger årlig

### KATEGORI B — Zenith-sektor brukt indirekte (geodetisk måling)

#### EKV-02 Oyambaro (Yaruquí-baselinjens nordende)
**Status**: INDIREKTE BEVIS — La Condamine brukte zenith-sektor for breddegrad

**Bevis**:
- La Condamine plasserte pyramiden i 1736 ved et endepunkt av baselinjen på Yaruquí-platået ([Rodama1789 — The Pyramids of Yaruqui](http://rodama1789.blogspot.com/2019/10/the-pyramids-of-yaruqui.html))
- "Rather than measure a star's altitude above the horizon, the zenith sector measures the difference between the star's position and a point directly overhead. The long stationery arm of the sector would be aligned along a vertical axis... by taking readings at different points on the north-south line the difference in latitude between them was determined." ([Rodama1789 — French scientists at the Equator 1735](http://rodama1789.blogspot.com/2019/10/la-condamine.html))
- "On the Palmar promontory, La Condamine patriotically marked the point at which the equator crosses the coastline of Peru, as determined by the astronomical observations of the Royal Academy of Sciences Paris" (samme kilde)

**Viktig nyanse**: Selve Oyambaro-pyramiden var et BASELINJE-endepunkt, ikke en ekvator-markør i seg selv. Den lå nord for nullinjen. Zenith-sektoren ble brukt til å BEREGNE breddegraden, ikke som direkte zenith-observasjons-markør.
**Kildevurdering**: Middels — historisk geodesi-monument, men ikke direkte zenith-bevis for selve punktet

---

#### EKV-03 Caraburo (Yaruquí-baselinjens sørende)
**Status**: INDIREKTE BEVIS — samme som Oyambaro

**Bevis**:
- "La Condamine decided to mark the two ends of the base with a millstone and a silver blade enclosed in a pyramide. The building of the pyramids was initiated in May 1740." ([CRW Flags — Yaruquí](https://www.crwflags.com/fotw/flags/ec-p-yar.html))
- "The Oyambaro pyramid can be visited today; the one in Caraburo stands on land now belonging to Quito International airport." ([Rodama1789 — The Pyramids of Yaruqui](http://rodama1789.blogspot.com/2019/10/the-pyramids-of-yaruqui.html))
- Baselinjen var 6272 toiser (≈12 km) og lå nord for ekvator-linjen ([Wikisource — Encyclopædia Britannica 9th ed.](https://en.wikisource.org/wiki/Page:Encyclop%C3%A6dia_Britannica,_Ninth_Edition,_v._7.djvu/620))

**Viktig nyanse**: Samme som Oyambaro — pyramidene markerte ENDEPUNKTENE av en trianguleringsbaselinje, ikke selve ekvator-passeringen
**Kildevurdering**: Middels — historisk monument, men ikke et zenith-markørpunkt

### KATEGORI C — Symbolsk monument, IKKE på ekvator

#### EKV-04 Mitad del Mundo (det store monumentet, 1979-1982)
**Status**: SVAKT BEVIS — feilplassert med 240 meter, ingen direkte zenith-observasjon

**Bevis mot**:
- "the embarrassing truth emerged: The Equator actually, and without a doubt, crosses the highway just up the road" ([Smithsonian Magazine — Much Ado About Nothing at the Equator](https://www.smithsonianmag.com/travel/much-ado-about-nothing-at-the-equator-8514125/))
- "according to readings based on the World Geodetic System WGS84... the equator actually lies about 240 metres (790 ft) north of the marked line." ([Wikipedia — Ciudad Mitad del Mundo](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ciudad_Mitad_del_Mundo))
- "Clearly the monument at Ciudad Mitad del Mundo is a tourist trap and a complete scam, given the very large margin of error in 240 meters" ([William & Mary — Latitude Zero](https://chuckbaileyrocks.pages.wm.edu/2025/09/23/latitude-zero-musings-on-the-equator/))

**Vurdering**: Dette monumentet ble bygget for å HEDRE den franske geodetiske misjonen, ikke som zenith-observasjonspunkt. Det ligger ikke engang på ekvator. Bør NEDPRIORITERES eller flyttes til "symbolsk minne-monument".
**Kildevurdering**: Svak som ekvator-markør — sterk som historisk minnesmerke

## Bonusfunn: Quitsato Sundial (0.00000°N, 78.17500°W)

**Status**: VERIFISERT zenith-bruk, men moderne (bygget 2006)

**Bevis**:
- "At midday in Quito on each equinox, the sun will be directly above the 10m hollow post and shine directly into the post, casting no shadow, but lighting up the inside of the gnomon." ([Border Sundials — Quitsato](https://www.bordersundials.co.uk/quitsato-sundial-el-reloj-solar-quitsato/))
- "precisely positioned on latitude 0°00′00′′, determined with an accuracy of 1 mm thanks to modern GPS measurements" ([TripAdvisor — Quitsato](https://www.tripadvisor.com/Attraction_Review-g940020-d4219214-Reviews-Quitsato_Sundial-Cayambe_Pichincha_Province.html))
- Koordinater: [Wikipedia — Quitsato Sundial](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Quitsato_Sundial)

**Vurdering**: Moderne demonstrasjon av zenith-prinsippet, ikke en gammel markør — men kan brukes som teknisk referansepunkt

## Konklusjon for Enoks modell

**To punkter** har solid, direkte zenith-bevis:
- **Catequilla** (Sør-Amerika, før-Columbiansk gnomon med dokumentert ingen-skygge ved jevndøgn)
- **Pontianak Tugu Khatulistiwa** (Sørøst-Asia, dokumentert solkulminasjon to ganger årlig)

Disse to alene gir empirisk bekreftelse på at solen står 90° rett opp ved ekvator ved jevndøgn — observert fra to ulike kontinenter, fra ulike kulturer, til ulike tider, uavhengig av hverandre.
