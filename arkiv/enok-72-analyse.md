## DEL A — Rammeverket (vers 1–5)

### 72:1
> "The book of the courses of the luminaries of the heaven, the relations of each, according to their classes, their dominion and their seasons, according to their names and places of origin, and according to their months, which Uriel, the holy angel, who was with me, who is their guide, showed me; and he showed me all their laws exactly as they are, and how it is with regard to all the years of the world"

**Innhold:** Tittelvers. Uriel er guide. Lovene gjelder "all the years of the world".
**Til regnearket:** Forklarer kildens autoritet. Ingen tallverdi.

### 72:2
> "and unto eternity, till the new creation is accomplished which dureth till eternity. And this is the first law of the luminaries: the luminary the Sun has its rising in the eastern portals of the heaven,"

**Innhold:** Solen står opp i **østlige porter**.
**Til regnearket:** Bekrefter at oppgang-porter er øst-portene (kol AS / port 4–6 i øst-sektor av Enok-modellen vår).

### 72:3
> "and its setting in the western portals of the heaven. And I saw six portals in which the sun rises, and six portals in which the sun sets and the moon rises and sets in these portals, and the leaders of the stars and those whom they lead: six in the east and six in the west, and all following each other"

**Innhold:** **6 østporter + 6 vestporter = 12 totalt**. Solen, månen, stjerneledere bruker alle disse portene.
**Til regnearket:** Definerer port-nummerering 1–6. Også bekreftet i topptekst rad 14: Port 1, Port 2, Port 3, Port 4, Port 5, Port 6.
**Til modellen vår:** 6 porter i øst-sektor (kl 12→3), 6 porter i vest-sektor (kl 6→9). Ingen porter i nord eller sør.

### 72:4
> "in accurately corresponding order: also many windows to the right and left of these portals. And first there goes forth the great luminary, named the Sun, and his circumference is like the"

**Innhold:** "Many windows to the right and left of the portals". Solens **omkrets = himmelens omkrets**.
**Til regnearket:** Vinduene = de små luke-åpningene i hver port (jf. 72:7 om 12 vinduer i 4. port).

### 72:5
> "circumference of the heaven, and he is quite filled with illuminating and heating fire. The chariot on which he ascends, the wind drives, and the sun goes down from the heaven and returns through the north in order to reach the east, and is so guided that he comes to the appropriate (lit. 'that') portal and"

**Innhold:** **Solens vogn drives av vinden**. Etter solnedgang **vender solen tilbake gjennom nord** for å nå østen.
**Til modellen vår:** ★ KRITISK ★ Dette bekrefter at solens nattbane går gjennom **nord-sektoren** (Sektor 4), gjennom diamanten (vår kjente verden) for å komme tilbake øst. Dette er grunnen til at diamantens nordspiss treffer Polaris — solen må passere bak Polaris om natten.

## DEL B — Månedene 1–6 (vår-sommer, solen stiger)

### 72:6
> "shines in the face of the heaven. In this way he rises in the first month in the great portal, which"

**Innhold:** **Måned 1 (Abib): solen står opp i den store porten**.

### 72:7
> "is the fourth [those six portals in the east]. And in that fourth portal from which the sun rises in the first month are twelve window-openings, from which proceed a flame when they are opened in"

**Innhold:** **Måned 1 = Port 4**. Port 4 har **12 vinduåpninger** med flamme.
**Regneark blokk 1:** Abib 1 = rad 122, Sat Mar 21, dag 1. Kolonne AP/AR = sol-port = 4. ✓

### 72:8
> "their season. When the sun rises in the heaven, he comes forth through that fourth portal thirty,"

**Innhold:** **30 morgener i Port 4** måned 1.

### 72:9
> "mornings in succession, and sets accurately in the fourth portal in the west of the heaven. And during this period the day becomes daily longer and the night nightly shorter to the thirtieth"

**Innhold:** Setter også i 4. vestport. Dagen blir lengre, natten kortere gjennom de 30 morgenene.

### 72:10
> "morning. On that day the day is longer than the night by a ninth part, and the day amounts exactly to ten parts and the night to eight parts. And the sun rises from that fourth portal, and sets in the fourth and returns to the fifth portal of the east thirty mornings, and rises from it and sets in the fifth"

**Innhold:**
- **Slutt på måned 1 (30. morgen):** dag = 10 deler, natt = 8 deler (av 18 totalt = 24t)
- **Måned 2 (Siv): 30 morgener i Port 5**
**Regneark:** Siv 30 = rad 151, dag 60. Siv 1 = rad 152 (R152: '2 SIV day 1 = tue Apr 20'). Port 5. ✓

### 72:11 *(mangler i kilde, men gir seg selv av kontekst)*
> Forventet: Beskrivelse av Port 5-overgangen.

### 72:12
> "portal. And then the day becomes longer by two parts and amounts to eleven parts, and the night"

**Innhold:** **Slutt på måned 2:** dag = 11 deler, natt = 7 deler.

### 72:13
> "becomes shorter and amounts to seven parts. And it returns to the east and enters into the sixth"

**Innhold:** **Måned 3 (Sivan): Port 6**.
**Regneark:** Sivan 1 = rad 182 (Thur May 20, dag 61). Port 6. ✓

### 72:14
> "portal, and rises and sets in the sixth portal one-and-thirty mornings on account of its sign. On that day the day becomes longer than the night, and the day becomes double the night, and the day"

**Innhold:**
- **Måned 3 = 31 morgener** (ikke 30) på grunn av "its sign" (= sommersolverv, Krepsens tegn)
- **Slutt på måned 3:** dag = dobbelt så lang som natt
**Regneark:** Sivan 31 = rad 212, Fri Jun 19, dag 91, "Krepsens Vendekrets 23.4°N". ✓

### 72:15
> "becomes twelve parts, and the night is shortened and becomes six parts. And the sun mounts up to make the day shorter and the night longer, and the sun returns to the east and enters into the"

**Innhold:**
- Sommersolverv: dag = 12 deler, natt = 6 deler
- Etter dette: solen begynner å vandre tilbake (dagen blir kortere)

### 72:16
> "sixth portal, and rises from it and sets thirty mornings. And when thirty mornings are accomplished,"

**Innhold:** **Måned 4 (Tammuz): 30 morgener tilbake i Port 6** (samme port som måned 3, men nedover).

### 72:17
> "the day decreases by exactly one part, and becomes eleven parts, and the night seven. And the sun goes forth from that sixth portal in the west, and goes to the east and rises in the fifth portal for"

**Innhold:**
- **Slutt på måned 4:** dag = 11, natt = 7
- **Måned 5 (Av): Port 5**

### 72:18
> "thirty mornings, and sets in the west again in the fifth western portal. On that day the day decreases by two parts, and amounts to ten parts and the night to eight parts. And the sun goes forth from that fifth portal and sets in the fifth portal of the west, and rises in the fourth portal for one-"

**Innhold:**
- **Måned 5 = 30 morgener i Port 5**, slutt: dag = 10, natt = 8
- **Måned 6 (Elul): Port 4**

### 72:19 *(mangler i kilde — beskrivelse av Port 4 fortsetter inn i 72:20)*

### 72:20
> "and-thirty mornings on account of its sign, and sets in the west. On that day the day is equalized with the night, [and becomes of equal length], and the night amounts to nine parts and the day to"

**Innhold:**
- **Måned 6 = 31 morgener** (igjen "on account of its sign" = høstjevndøgn)
- **Slutt på måned 6:** dag = natt = 9 deler hver

## KALIBRERING TIL 2026

Vårjevndøgn = **fredag 20. mars 2026** (siste dag av Adar = Adar 31, "its sign").
Abib 1 = **lørdag 21. mars 2026** = årets dag 1 = Port 4, dag-fase 10/8.

## HVA SOM IKKE STÅR I ENOK 72

Enok 72 sier IKKE:
- Hvor mange grader hver port spenner over (regnearkets "Grader Øst/Vest 0–180" er en avledning, ikke direkte tekst)
- Eksakt vinkel mellom portene
- Hvor høyt over horisonten portene ligger
- Detaljer om "many windows" til høyre/venstre av portene (bare antall: 12 i Port 4)

Enok 72 sier TYDELIG:
- Solens bane går gjennom **nord** etter solnedgang (72:5) — ★ kritisk for modellen vår ★
- Sol og måne har **lik diameter** (72:37) — ★ allerede inne ★
- Året er **364 dager eksakt** (72:33)
- **12 porter totalt: 6 øst + 6 vest** (72:3)
- **12 vinduer i Port 4** (72:7) — uavklart om de andre portene også har vinduer
- "Many windows to the right and left of these portals" (72:4) — det er flere vinduer enn bare portene

## NESTE STEG FOR SIMULATOREN

1. **Solens posisjon hver dag** beregnes ut fra:
   - Hvilken port den står opp i (tabellen over)
   - Hvor langt inn i måneden den er (dag 1–31)
   - Dagslyslengde i deler (lineær interpolering mellom månedsstart og månedsslutt)

2. **Visuelt på modellen:**
   - Solen vises som en sirkel i øst-sektor (oppgang) eller vest-sektor (nedgang)
   - Mellom oppgang og nedgang: bue over himmelen, høyde avhengig av måned
   - Om natten: solen passerer gjennom nord-sektoren (bak diamanten)

3. **Dag/natt-fase i timer:**
   - 18 deler = 24 timer → 1 del = 1t 20min
   - Sommersolverv: 12 deler dag = 16 timer dagslys, 6 deler natt = 8 timer mørke
   - Vintersolverv: motsatt
   - Jevndøgn: 9+9 = 12+12 timer
